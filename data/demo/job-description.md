# Senior Backend Engineer (Platform)

**Company:** Northwind — a Series B fintech building payment infrastructure for marketplaces
**Location:** Remote (UK / EU time zones) · **Type:** Full-time

## About the role
We're hiring a Senior Backend Engineer to join the Platform team that owns the
services moving money for thousands of marketplaces. You'll design and operate
high-throughput, low-latency APIs, harden our payment ledger, and mentor
mid-level engineers. This is a hands-on role with real ownership.

## Must-have requirements
- 5+ years building backend services in production (Go, Java, or similar typed language)
- Strong experience designing and operating REST or gRPC APIs at scale
- Production experience with PostgreSQL — schema design, query optimisation, migrations
- Hands-on Kubernetes in production (deploying, debugging, scaling real workloads)
- CI/CD ownership — you've built and maintained pipelines, not just used them
- Solid grasp of distributed-systems fundamentals (idempotency, consistency, retries)

## Nice to have
- Fintech, payments, or ledger/accounting domain experience
- Terraform or other infrastructure-as-code
- Event-driven architecture (Kafka, NATS, or similar)
- Experience mentoring or leading a small team

## What you'll do
- Own backend services end to end — design, build, deploy, operate, and on-call
- Improve reliability and latency of the payment ledger and settlement pipeline
- Drive engineering standards: testing, observability, incident response
- Mentor two mid-level engineers and review their designs and code

## Screening priorities
For the first call, prioritise: depth of production Kubernetes experience, evidence
of owning services in production (not just contributing), PostgreSQL depth, and
signals of distributed-systems maturity. Payments domain is a strong plus but not
a blocker.
