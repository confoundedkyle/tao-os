# Scorecard — Senior Backend Engineer (Platform), Northwind

Score each candidate 1–5 per criterion (1 = clear gap, 3 = meets bar, 5 =
exceptional). Weighted criteria are the ones Dana cares most about; a low score
on a must-have is a likely no regardless of total.

## Tenure & signal check

## Recommendation
- **Strong yes:** ≥4 on all must-haves and ≥2 nice-to-haves above bar.
- **Yes:** all must-haves ≥3, no red flags.
- **No:** any must-have ≤2, or unexplained tenure red flags.
